Running Routes in St Andrews

2k to 4k Lade Braes

Find the start of Lade Braes on South Street (the alleyway going down to Regs) and just follow it right down to DRA and circle round back past Hepburn Gardens, you'll have finished 4km whilst spanning some good path running. On a nice day you'll have the sun coming down past the Lade Braes stream, down an easy terrain and no chance of getting too lost.

5k Parkrun

If competitive, or group running is more your thing be sure to check out the weekly timed parkrun in Craigtoun country park. A little bit out of town, but sure to blow away to cobwebs on a Suaturday morning and meet some faces others than those of students

5k Cannongate

However, if you'd prefer a more local 5k try the scenic and simple run along Canongate. Still taking you a little out of St Andrews, allowing minimal traffic but still staying within St Andrews boundaries

9km to 18km West Sands, all in

Want to feel like you are running in a movie? Run the length of West Sands and back and feel like you are in Chariots of Fire, because your lungs certainly will be...

21km Leuchars half-marathon

Want to get longer distances under your belt? There and back running to Leuchars is just over a half marathon. Good to mimic a typical road race with safe cycle paths the whole way, this track will get those legs going.
